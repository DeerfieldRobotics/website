Deerfield Robotics is committed to providing equal access to the content on its website. We have made and will make every effort to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines developed by the World Wide Web Consortium. Individuals who wish to request an alternate version of Deerfield Robotics' web content, ask questions regarding web accessibility or individuals who are unable to access any pages or documents on the Deerfield Robotics website, can email the webmaster at 261navickasm@student.dist113.org and indicate the nature of the accessibility or alternative format needs and the URL (web address) of the material.
