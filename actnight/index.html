Deerfield Robotics is a club at DHS that runs and manages three teams. These teams are a part of the FIRST Tech Challenge, the global league for robotics. If you want to join, or are even interested, add yourself to the interest form and we'll email you regarding the upcoming season. Even if you aren't that interested, we still recommend you come to the boot camp! Eligibility
All DHS Students Time Period
Sept-Nov: Planning, Design, and Build
Nov-Feb: League, Regional, and State Meets
Feb-May: Postseason (optional) Meetings
Tuesday-Thursday, 3:15-5:30 PM in J111
Recommended commitment level is 1-2 times/week
Meeting times are more frequent during week leading up to meets
Held in dedicated lab space Skills Required
None! If you have skills in CAD, design, Java, etc., that's great! However, we hold a three-day boot camp at the beginning of the season after school, and everyone will be taught the basics. Otherwise, team members learn through observation and working in the lab. Contacts
